DOES DIET PLAN MATTER?


Indeed. Diet plays more vital role in formation of a good body over just the exercise. As said, If you can control your hunger, eat healthy and avoid junk, a run alone could help you stay lean. Most people often ask what to eat, when to eat, if that is good for them, if it has too much calories, what does per serving size is, so here is an answer to all the unanswered in a short table. (Individual results may vary, this diet plan was made for a woman with a height of 5'1 and weighing 50KGS.)

HOW TO BEGIN THE WORKOUT ?


If that has been your question for years, or if you are totally a begineer, here's a workout guide for you.
Start easy. Work in sets of 8 reps each.
This might seem intially too light exercise for some but always remember, the more you rush,
the higher chances you have of a muscle breakdown. Health is wealth. Make sure you play safe.



  1. Flat bench barbell press – 4 sets of 8 reps.
  2. Push ups – 4 sets of 10 reps.
  3. Cable crossovers – 3 sets of 15 reps.
  4. Incline dumbbell flyes – 4 sets of 12 reps.
  5. Barbell biceps curls – 3 sets of 15 reps.
  6. Alternate arm hammer curls – 4 sets of 12 reps per arm.
